Review and Follow-Up

Problem detection and course correction is needed for every individual when he is working on a task. We understand this. Regular reviews are done of the pupils. A progress bar is made of the student and understanding the nature of his questions identifies the problem area and he is suitably replied. Lots of students do not ask questions in class. The app supports them at this time. A special review is done for the student if the progress bar does not rise in the generally expected time.


Finishing Touches

This is the last leg of the course and is divided over a few hours every week. This includes the following: ! Mentoring for portfolio – How can the students portray their projects in the most effective manner. ! Mentoring for Technical Interview Preparation. ! Honing of Soft Skills through tips and mock interviews. ! Test Preparation (If Opted).
