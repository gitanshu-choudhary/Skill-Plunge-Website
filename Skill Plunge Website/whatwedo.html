What We Do ?

Our programs fill this void. We are offering a Six-Week Program to prepare the candidates to take on the job market head on. Ours will be a blended program that will focus on Project-Based Learning and Real-Time Problem Solving.

The course will have 6 modules. Every module shall take a week’s time. Each module has been designed in such a manner that after 6 weeks, the candidate will have 6 small projects and one major project at the end of the course.


Why We Doing This ?

With the mushrooming of engineering colleges all over India, there is cutthroat competition among colleges to get their students placed and among students to get the best output of their college education in the form of worthy jobs.

It has been observed through our internal assessments of placement data of quite a few engineering colleges, that several good employers, mass-recruiters and multi-million dollar companies come to engineering institutes to look for employable candidates but they end up giving a very high rejection rate and very little selection rate.

Why?
This is because the companies are seeking job-ready individuals BUT they end up finding qualified engineers with little experience of real life programming problems. It is therefore imperative that the students should be job-ready before they appear for job placement interviews.
