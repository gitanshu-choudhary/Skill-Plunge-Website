Course

Section A:

Classroom Based Learning

The Classroom promotes fundamental learning by Pen and Paper through pseudo-codes. In a highly interactive setup, our mentor shall teach the student how to reach the solution of a problem theoretically, building his fundamentals through our curated techniques. There shall be no coding in the class

At the end of the class, the mentor shall give a demo of homework – a one-day task, that shall be part of a fortnightly project, shall be given to the student and the demo shall highlight how the students are expected to achieve the required results. There shall be a maximum of 30 people per batch for increased attention. Then comes the next step – Blending classroom learning to App-Based learning.

Section B:

App Based Learning

Our App shall offer content not taught in class. It shall include guided step-by-step means to achieve the desired codes and results. The app shall give the student a ready reckoner for the problems that they would face and the solutions thereof. The daily homework, combined with in App MCQs shall help the students with their project.

In App Assessment will be done regularly and progress reports will be made and reflected to both the students and the mentors. The App will offer FAQs, an open chat forum and scheduled chat support to keep the student engaged at all times and never letting him feel helpless in any situation. Slowly but surely, the results will come.
