Skill Plunge

SkillPlunge is a skill development organization based out of NCR that is focusing on programs to make engineers job - ready. We at SkillPlunge have designed a unique blended learning style and structured program for engineering students who, after investing several Lakh Rupees and precious four-five years of their life, end up with either no jobs or jobs not befitting their expectations.
